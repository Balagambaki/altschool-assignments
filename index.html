Information Abou Me

I am by name Bala Muhammad Gambaki, from Azare, Katagum Local Government of Bauchi State. I base in the state capital, and I work as a Public Health Officer.

I am a student at AltSchool Africa in the School of Engineering, Frontend Engineering Track with the student ID ALT/SOE//025/3537.

My journey in the Frontend Engineering stated 2024 in Kano State, where I was a beneficiary of a Tech program, the program admitted students into 3 programs (Frontend Development, UI/UX Design, and Data Science and Machine Learning).
